Importance of plants

The importance of plants to humans and just about all other life on Earth is staggering.

Life as we know it would not be possible without plants.

Plants make our landscapes beautiful as without them the world would be a barren rock.

Why are plants so important?

  1. Plants provide many products for human use, such as firewood, timber, fibers and rubber.
  2. Plants create habitats for many organisms.
  3. Plants supply food to nearly all terrestrial organisms, including humans.
  4. Plants maintain the atmosphere. They produce oxygen and absorb carbon dioxide during photosynthesis.
